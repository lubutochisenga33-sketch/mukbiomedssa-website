About MUK-BIOMEDSSA

MUK-BIOMEDSSA (Makerere University Biomedical Sciences Students Association) is the official student association representing students enrolled in Biomedical Sciences programs at Makerere University.

Our mission is to foster academic excellence, professional development, and community among biomedical sciences students.

Through seminars, workshops, outreach programs, and social events, we build a strong community that prepares students to become competent, ethical, and innovative leaders in the biomedical sciences field.
